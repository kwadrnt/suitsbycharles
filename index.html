why do you choose Suits By Charles

Look Your Absolute Best for Any Occasion

Mission

You will look your absolute finest in business attire at your special engagement. Whether it will be applicable for business, leisure or wedding, you’ll be in the nicest luxury garment with the highest quality material to exceed dapper standards.

Description

Your confidence in how you look is our priority. Each client will be given a recommendation of the type of suit that is appropriate based off of the occasion it is needed for and/or skin tone. Post purchase, we will give you the best recommendation for the type of tailoring that you need after the purchase of your suit, based off of height, weight, and personal liking to ensure that you will look absolutely dapper in my line of luxury garments upon taking the suit to the tailor of your choice.

How to Buy

Suits By Charles is mobile with the purchase of two or more suits. Upon fitting (if needed) and ordering, your suit will be ready for pick-up/delivery within 5 business days (Monday-Friday).
